3 - 1 Words and sentences
=========================

Words and sentences are the parts where we start seeing meaning.

Languages differ in
- How sentences are made up
	- Turkish: Long word with a lot of meaning
	- Standard chinese: Lots of short words
- Organization of words
	- Dutch: the person who does something is the first word in the sentence
	-  Apalai: The person who does something is at the end of the sentence

Morphology: the study of the internal structure of words. How are words composed of smaller meaningful words

Syntax: How are sentences built from words

Even concepts like sentences and words are different. What is a word?
